# Twilio

- Category: email and communications
- Priority tier: tier 3
- Ideal use cases: SMS, voice, messaging workflows
- Setup requirements: account, messaging config, credentials
- Permissions or credentials: Twilio credentials and sender setup
- Supported workflows: SMS auth, messaging notifications, support workflows
- Validation steps: credentials valid, sender configured, test message succeeds
- Common failure modes: sender setup incomplete, geography or carrier restrictions ignored
- Related ORCA-HVN commands or docs: `orca-integration`, `integration-diagnostics.md`
- Related integrations: Firebase Auth, Clerk, Slack
- Risk notes: messaging cost and compliance matter
- Web, mobile, or platform caveats: communications layer, not a push-notification replacement
