# Zendesk

- Category: business and project systems
- Priority tier: tier 3
- Ideal use cases: support and issue handoff workflows
- Setup requirements: account access and chosen ticket handoff pattern
- Permissions or credentials: Zendesk access
- Supported workflows: support context capture and escalation linkage
- Validation steps: access works, ticket context path documented
- Common failure modes: support evidence lost when not mapped into product work
- Related ORCA Framework commands or docs: `orca-integration`
- Related integrations: Linear, Slack, Productboard
- Risk notes: support system should not become an unstructured bug backlog
- Web, mobile, or platform caveats: business workflow surface
