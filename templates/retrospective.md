# Retrospective

## Work Completed

Summarize the shipped or completed work.

## Outcome Against Spec

Compare result to goals and acceptance criteria.

## What Worked

List practices, tools, or decisions worth repeating.

## What Failed Or Slowed Work

List causes, not only symptoms.

## Surprises

Record unexpected technical, product, or workflow discoveries.

## Follow-Ups

List concrete actions with owner, trigger, or next command.

## Framework Improvements

List changes HVN itself should consider.
