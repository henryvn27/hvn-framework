# Writing Calibration

## Task

State the writing deliverable.

## Audience

Name who will read it and what they need from it.

## Voice

Describe the target tone, rhythm, vocabulary, and level of directness.

## Generic Patterns To Avoid

List filler, vague claims, predictable structure, and phrases to avoid.

## Grounding Details

List specific examples, constraints, references, product facts, or arguments to include.

## Point Of View

State what the piece should clearly believe, prioritize, or reject.

## Review Criteria

State how review should judge specificity, credibility, rhythm, and polish.
