# QA Brief Contract

## Required Fields

- Work item or issue
- QA mode: blind, briefed, or informed
- Included context
- Withheld context
- Target flows
- Launch instructions
- Evidence expectations

## Optional Fields

- Known risk areas
- Accessibility focus

## Good Enough

- The tester knows exactly what context is allowed.
- The brief preserves the integrity of the intended QA mode.
