# Idea Guided Mode

Ask for these in order:

1. Who is the customer?
2. What painful problem do they have today?
3. How do they solve it now?
4. What is the proposed wedge?
5. Why now?
6. What is the strongest founder or team angle?
7. What is the biggest assumption?
8. What is the next validation step?

Stop once a usable one-pager exists.
