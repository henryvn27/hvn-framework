# Editorial Profile

## Visual Tone

Calm, text-led, spacious, and restrained.

## Layout Tendencies

Use strong reading columns, margin notes, understated dividers, and occasional asymmetry for emphasis.

## Typography Tendencies

Editorial serif or humanist sans, generous line height, careful measure, few weights.

## Motion Tendencies

Minimal. Use motion for reveal or orientation only.

## Writing Tone

Clear, argued, specific, and lightly opinionated.

## Anti-Patterns To Avoid

Avoid hype language, dense card grids, decorative gradients, and shallow pull quotes.

## Trust And Brand Cues

Specific examples, evidence, direct claims, and calm hierarchy.

## Calibration Influence

Writing calibration should prioritize rhythm and point of view. Design calibration should protect reading quality.
