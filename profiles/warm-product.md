# Warm Product Profile

## Visual Tone

Approachable, practical, and quietly polished.

## Layout Tendencies

Use open layouts, soft but not over-rounded surfaces, clear task paths, and human-scale spacing.

## Typography Tendencies

Friendly sans with restrained display moments and readable body rhythm.

## Motion Tendencies

Gentle state feedback. Avoid constant animation.

## Writing Tone

Plainspoken, helpful, and concrete.

## Anti-Patterns To Avoid

Avoid sentimental copy, beige sameness, generic onboarding illustrations, and hollow friendliness.

## Trust And Brand Cues

Clear recovery states, specific next actions, and copy that respects the user's time.

## Calibration Influence

Design calibration should make the product feel usable before it feels charming. Writing calibration should stay warm without becoming vague.
