# Brutalist Profile

## Visual Tone

Direct, mechanical, high-contrast, and structurally exposed.

## Layout Tendencies

Use hard edges, visible grids, dense labels, raw dividers, and deliberate imbalance.

## Typography Tendencies

Condensed sans, mono labels, large utility type, minimal typographic decoration.

## Motion Tendencies

Sharp and functional. Prefer snap and state changes over ambient motion.

## Writing Tone

Terse, concrete, and operational.

## Anti-Patterns To Avoid

Avoid soft gradients, glass panels, rounded SaaS cards, vague emotional copy, and decorative blur.

## Trust And Brand Cues

Evidence, timestamps, status labels, command language, and visible system structure.

## Calibration Influence

Design calibration should favor raw hierarchy and utility. Writing calibration should remove polish that weakens urgency.
