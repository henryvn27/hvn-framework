# Systems Dashboard Profile

## Visual Tone

Operational, dense, legible, and status-oriented.

## Layout Tendencies

Use panels, tables, timelines, status rails, compact filters, and hierarchy based on risk.

## Typography Tendencies

Readable sans, mono data labels, compact captions, and clear numeric hierarchy.

## Motion Tendencies

Use motion to show refresh, transition, selection, or alert state.

## Writing Tone

Precise, low-drama, and action-oriented.

## Anti-Patterns To Avoid

Avoid marketing heroes, oversized empty cards, decorative charts, and equal-weight widgets.

## Trust And Brand Cues

Fresh timestamps, source labels, clear severity, audit trails, and exact actions.

## Calibration Influence

Design calibration should optimize scanning and comparison. Writing calibration should name states and actions without narrative fog.
