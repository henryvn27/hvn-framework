# Code Of Conduct

ORCA-HVN follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, direct, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be specific and factual in technical feedback.
- Separate critique of work from critique of people.
- Assume good intent while still addressing concrete harm or risk.
- Respect boundaries, identities, and lived experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Personal attacks or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants when behavior undermines a safe and productive project environment.

Report concerns privately to the maintainer listed in `SECURITY.md`.
