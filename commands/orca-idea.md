# orca-idea

## Purpose

Turn a rough startup or business concept into a structured ORCA-HVN ideation artifact.

## When To Use

Use when the user has an early idea, rough wedge, market hunch, or company concept that needs structure before evaluation or build planning.

## Required Inputs

- Rough idea or opportunity statement

## Optional Inputs

- Founder or team context
- Market or customer guess
- Known competitors
- Desired outcome

## Workflow

1. Use `orca-business-ideation`.
2. Capture the idea in `templates/idea-intake.md`.
3. Compress it into `templates/idea-one-pager.md`.
4. Surface the biggest assumptions and open questions.
5. Recommend whether evaluation, research, or validation should come next.

## Side-Session Fit

`orca-idea` is a strong side-session candidate because brainstorming, naming, and market framing should stay separate from execution until the idea is strong enough to hand off into spec or research.

## Outputs And Artifacts

- `templates/idea-intake.md`
- `templates/idea-one-pager.md`

## Failure Cases

- If the customer or problem is too vague, keep the output at intake level and do not force false precision.
- If the idea is already far enough along for product work, route to `orca-spec` instead of re-brainstorming.

## Related Commands And Skills

- Commands: `orca-evaluate-idea`, `orca-plan-idea`, `orca-validate-idea`, `orca-spec`
- Skills: `orca-business-ideation`
