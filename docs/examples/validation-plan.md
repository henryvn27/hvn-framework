# Example Validation Plan

## Decision To Support

Whether event operators care enough about vendor-intake chaos to pay for a focused solution.

## Key Assumptions To Test

- the workflow pain is among the top three admin headaches
- operators will pay for relief
- the first wedge can exclude payments and still matter

## Chosen Validation Method

Problem interviews followed by a manual concierge pilot offer.

## Smallest Credible Experiment

Interview six operators, then offer two of them a paid or high-commitment manual onboarding service.

## Success Signal

Multiple operators rank the problem as urgent and at least one accepts a serious pilot offer.

## Failure Signal

Operators describe the problem as minor, already solved, or not worth budget attention.

## Mixed Result Interpretation

If pain is strong but payment resistance is high, narrow the wedge further or change the buyer.

## Timebox

Two weeks.

## Owner

Founder.

## Next Action After Results

Either write the first product spec or park the idea.
