# Example Idea Kill Decision

## Decision

Kill.

## Why

The problem sounded fashionable but interviews suggested the target users do not experience enough urgency to change behavior or pay.

## What We Believe

Some users find the concept interesting, but the operational pain is too weak and alternatives are already good enough.

## What We Do Not Yet Know

Whether a different customer segment would care more.

## Trigger To Revisit

Only revisit if a new segment appears with stronger pain and existing distribution access.

## Immediate Next Action

Stop planning product work and move to a stronger idea.
