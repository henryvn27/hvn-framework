# Example: High-Confidence Quality Issue Approved

The session showed three strong signals:

- repeated correction by the user
- repeated low-specificity output
- continued wrong-direction framing after correction

Why it warranted a framework issue:

- the pattern was reusable
- the likely source area was clear
- the evidence was concrete
- fixing it would improve future sessions

Outcome:

ORCA Framework asked for approval, prepared a structured draft, and linked the likely framework areas to update.
