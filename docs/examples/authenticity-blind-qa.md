# Example: Blind QA Flags Generic Feel

## Context Received

URL only. No spec, source code, or design rationale.

## First Impression

The site looks polished but indistinct. The first viewport uses a familiar gradient background, a broad productivity claim, and three feature cards. I cannot infer what makes this product different from other SaaS tools.

## Product Risk

Generic first impression reduces trust. The page may be visually complete, but it does not communicate product specificity or a credible point of view.

## Recommendation

Run authenticity preflight again with real product evidence, sharper audience definition, and a ban on reusable startup-page structure.
