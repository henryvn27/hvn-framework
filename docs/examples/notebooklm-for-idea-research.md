# NotebookLM For Idea Research

NotebookLM can be a strong fit when idea research involves large source sets such as competitor writeups, public filings, technical references, customer interview notes, or market reports.

Good ORCA Framework behavior:

- use NotebookLM to compress and question the source set
- keep evidence and inference separate
- move from synthesis to a concrete validation decision
- avoid treating NotebookLM output as proof by itself
