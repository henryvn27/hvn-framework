# NotebookLM Research Flow

Use NotebookLM when the user already has a notebook full of product docs, competitor decks, support notes, or technical references and wants ORCA-HVN to help synthesize it.

Recommended ORCA-HVN behavior:

- confirm that the work is research-heavy
- confirm whether the user is on an enterprise or community integration path
- treat NotebookLM as the knowledge layer, not the stack default
- produce a short research summary, evidence gaps, and next validation questions
