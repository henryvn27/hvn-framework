# Example Idea One-Pager

## Idea Name

Scoutly Vendor Intake

## One-Sentence Description

A lightweight intake tool for small event operators who currently coordinate vendors through messy text threads, PDFs, and spreadsheets.

## Target Customer

Independent event operators running recurring local festivals with 20 to 150 vendors.

## Painful Problem

Vendor onboarding is repetitive, deadline-sensitive, and full of missing documents, payment confusion, and status ambiguity.

## Current Alternatives

Email, Google Forms, spreadsheets, generic project tools, and manual follow-up.

## Proposed Solution

A focused workflow that collects required vendor information, tracks status, and surfaces missing steps before event week.

## Why Now

Operators already tolerate software sprawl, and AI-assisted admin tooling lowers the cost of shipping a narrow workflow product.

## Founder Or Team Angle

The team already knows event-operations workflows and has direct access to early pilot users.

## Major Assumptions

- operators will pay for reduced administrative chaos
- the wedge is narrow enough to win
- vendor-side adoption friction is manageable

## Open Questions

- which workflow step hurts most
- whether payments should be included initially
- whether seasonality creates retention problems

## Next Validation Step

Run six operator interviews focused on document collection, deadline tracking, and willingness to pay for reduced follow-up work.
