# Blind-To-Briefed Delta Example

## Blind Result

The tester inferred the app generated meeting briefs but could not find saved templates. They tried the settings area and abandoned the task.

## Briefed Result

After receiving the mission "create and use a saved template," the tester found templates under the compose menu and completed the task.

## Delta Insight

The feature works after briefing, but the entry point is not discoverable. The problem is information architecture and action labeling, not core functionality.

## Ship Recommendation

Do not ship without clearer template entry points or onboarding copy.
