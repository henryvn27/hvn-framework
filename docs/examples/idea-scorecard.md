# Example Idea Scorecard

## Founder Or Team Fit

- Rating: strong
- Notes: direct user access and existing workflow knowledge create a credible starting edge.

## Problem Quality

- Rating: medium-strong
- Notes: the pain is real and recurring, but must be shown to be expensive enough to pay for.

## Market Quality

- Rating: medium
- Notes: likely fragmented and not obviously venture-scale from the first wedge.

## Competitive Landscape

- Rating: medium
- Notes: many generic workflow alternatives exist, but few are purpose-built for this exact intake process.

## Mechanism And Execution Logic

- Rating: strong
- Notes: the workflow improvement path is understandable and operationally plausible.

## Defensibility Or Asymmetry

- Rating: medium-weak
- Notes: strong near-term wedge, weak long-term moat unless deeper workflow embedment develops.

## Overall Judgment

This looks like a real operational pain with a credible founder angle, but the market-size and defensibility case is still incomplete.
