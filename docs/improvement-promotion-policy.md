# Improvement Promotion Policy

Promotion is the bridge between local observations and framework-level change.

## Promotion Requires

- recurrence
- evidence across sessions or users
- clear generalizability
- actionability
- non-duplicate status

## Non-Promotion Cases

Do not promote when:

- the signal is weak
- the pattern is user-specific
- the likely fix is unclear
- the candidate is already tracked without new evidence
