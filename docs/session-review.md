# Session Review

The session-end review should be short.

It exists to capture actionable framework friction while the evidence is still fresh, not to produce a long essay.

## Questions

Ask:

- what slowed progress?
- what was confusing?
- what repeated manual effort?
- what should have been easier?
- what should ORCA-HVN have already known or handled?
- what feature, doc, guide, command, or automation was missing?
- was this a one-off or a reusable framework problem?

## Output Shape

Use [templates/session-improvement-review.md](../templates/session-improvement-review.md).

The review should fit on one screen in most cases.

## Trigger Rule

Prefer the review after meaningful sessions, blocked sessions, or surprising sessions.

Do not force it after every trivial success.
