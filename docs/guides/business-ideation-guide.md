# Business Ideation Guide

## When To Use This Guide

Use this lane before a product idea has earned a delivery spec.

## How It Works

The ideation lane turns a rough idea into:

- an idea one-pager
- a scorecard
- an opportunity memo
- a validation plan
- a decision to continue or stop

## Common Pitfalls

- writing implementation plans before the idea is pressure-tested
- treating speculation as evidence
- skipping a concrete validation step
