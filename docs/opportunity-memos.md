# Opportunity Memos

An opportunity memo is the synthesis artifact for ORCA-HVN ideation. It turns scattered thinking into a decision-ready document.

## Purpose

Use an opportunity memo to summarize:

- what the idea is
- why the problem matters
- what the strongest positive signals are
- what the major risks are
- which claims are still weakly evidenced
- what type of opportunity this looks like
- what should happen next

## Output Types

The memo should classify the opportunity honestly, for example:

- venture-scale candidate
- strong niche or lifestyle business
- unclear but promising wedge
- crowded or weakly differentiated
- not worth pursuing now

## Relationship To Decisions

The memo is the narrative layer. The decision artifact is the action layer.

Use:

- [templates/opportunity-memo.md](../templates/opportunity-memo.md)
- [templates/idea-decision.md](../templates/idea-decision.md)

The decision should explicitly say:

- pursue
- park
- kill
- revise and retest
